About PSAs Against Plastic

Our Mission

PSAs Against Plastic is dedicated to reducing plastic pollution in Delaware and surrounding areas through the use of media announcements, digital resources, and organized cleanup days.

What Are PSAs?

Public service announcements (PSAs) are brief messages that air on media stations with the goal of informing the audience about certain issues, as well as acting as a call to action for the general public. To view our PSAs, click here.

Who Are We?

PSAs Against Plastic is a community service project through the Delaware 4-H Diamond Clover program, created by a member of the Sunset Branch 4-H Club in Dagsboro, Delaware.
For information about Delaware 4-H, visit the Delaware 4-H foundation or the Delaware 4-H Cooperative Extension. To learn about the Diamond Clover program, click here and scroll to "Diamond Clover 4-H Award".
